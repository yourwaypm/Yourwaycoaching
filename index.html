Who this coaching is actually for.

This isn’t a motivation seminar. It’s for people who want to be in the arena or are already there and want their operation cleaner, stronger, and more profitable — whether you’re just starting out or leveling up.

  • Property managers with 10–150 doors
  • Agents with real, active clients
  • Hands-on investors & flippers
  • People willing to implement between calls

You don’t need another “mindset hack.” You need clear priorities, a small number of systems that actually run, and someone who will call you out when you’re avoiding the real work.

Every call is focused on decisions, implementation, and accountability. Less theory. More “here’s what you do this week.”

Not for you if…

  • You’re hoping for a magic funnel that fixes everything while you coast.
  • You won’t touch a phone, email, or conversation with owners, tenants, buyers or contractors.
  • You want motivation more than you want responsibility and execution.
  • You collect courses but rarely implement anything past week one.

This is for you if…

  • You’re already doing deals, managing doors, or running a real estate business.
  • You’re willing to show the ugly parts: broken processes, bad leads, messy numbers, rough projects.
  • You want blunt, practical feedback more than you want to be told you’re “crushing it.”
  • You’re ready to trade chaos for systems, even if it means changing habits.

FAQ: before we get on a call.

Read this first so you know what you’re walking into and what you’re not.

How long is the commitment?

Most clients work in 3 month blocks. That’s enough time to build, test, and refine systems. If you just want a single deep-dive strategy session, that’s possible too.

Do you work with beginners?

Yes. I work with beginners and experienced operators. If you’re brand new, you just need to be serious about taking action: talking to people, looking at deals, running numbers, or getting your first project moving. If you’re only “thinking about real estate someday,” this isn’t the best use of your money yet.

What happens on the calls?

We look at what’s actually happening in your business: leads, deals, doors, bottlenecks, projects, numbers. Then we pick the highest-leverage problems and build simple, executable steps for the next 1–2 weeks.

Is this just templates and theory?

You’ll get templates, scripts, checklists and frameworks, but they’re there to support decisions, not replace them. The focus stays on building a business that works in your world, not just on paper.
