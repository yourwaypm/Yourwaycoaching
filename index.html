FAQ

Questions you’re probably already asking.

Short answers, no spin. If something isn’t covered here, drop it in the contact form and we’ll talk about your specific mess.

How do the coaching calls work? +
We meet on Zoom, typically every other week. Sessions run about 45–60 minutes. You bring what’s on fire plus any numbers, screenshots, or documents you want reviewed. We prioritize, troubleshoot, and leave you with a short list of concrete next steps.
Is there a contract or can I cancel? +
No long-term lock-in. Coaching is month-to-month. If you’re doing the work and not getting value, we end it like adults and move on.
Do you only work with people in Rochester? +
No. Rochester is home base, but coaching is available anywhere as long as you can meet online and provide the info we need (rent rolls, P&L, deals, etc.).
Will you look at my deals or my actual PM company? +
Yes. That’s kind of the point. We can review your deals, systems, lease language, owner mix, or whatever is actually causing you pain right now and work through it together.
Is this tax deductible? +
In many cases, professional coaching and education for your business can be treated as an expense. Talk with your tax professional for your specific situation. This is not tax advice, etc., you know the drill.
